What do you do?

Science Works is a research and policy studio focused on accelerating progress in British science and technology.

We work on building and improving the organisational, technological, and intellectual infrastructure that the UK's research and innovation system needs to stay at the frontier. We work on a small number of identified problems that we believe, if solved, will generate the highest leverage and impact for both the UK and for humanity as a whole.

Science Works is editorially independent, strictly non-partisan, not-for-profit, and not aligned with any single funder, department, agency, or institution.
